Recipe: Ninfa's Green Sauce}

Ingredients List:

  • 3 medium-size green tomatoes, coarsely chopped
  • 4 tomatillos, cleaned and chopped
  • 1 to 2 jalapenos, stemmed and coarsely chopped
  • 3 small garlic cloves
  • 3 medium-size ripe avocados
  • 4 springs cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 c. sour cream

Recipe Steps:

  1. 1. Combine chopped tomatoes, tomatillos, jalapenos and garlic in a saucepan.
  2. 2. Bring to a boil (tomatoes provide all the liquid), reduce heat and simmer 10-15 minutes or until tomatoes are soft.
  3. 3. Peel, pit, and slice avocados; set aside.
  4. 4. Place tomato mixture with part of the avocados.
  5. 5. Place tomato mixture with part of the avocados, cilantro and salt in food process and process until smooth.
  6. 6. Turn into a large bowl and stil in sour cream.
  7. 7. Makes 4-5 cups and refrigerate leftovers
