Recipe: Ninfa's Tacos Al Carbon

Ingredients List:

  • 3 pounds outside skirt steak or flatiron steak
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp garlic
  • 1/2 c. soy sauce
  • 4 springs cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 c. sour cream

Recipe Steps:

  1. 1. Combine marinade ingredients in a large bowl or large resealable plastic bag. Add the meat, turn to coat all sides, and let marinade refrigerated for at least 30 minutes or as long as 3 hours. Let meat come to room temperature before grilling.
  2. 2. Heat grill to 400 degrees (the meat also can be cooked on a grill pan on the stove top). Place meat on grill. Cook meat for 7 minutes on each side to medium rare (10 minutes each side for medium / medium well).
  3. 3. Remove meat from grill and let rest for 5 minutes. Slice meat across the grain. Place meat in tortillas and serve with your favorite pico de gallo, salsa, guacamole, rice, beans and chile con queso.
